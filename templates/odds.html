Race Night Call

In Development

We are building a proprietary race-night election calling model using Stata statistical software. Given the high volume of prediction market data and the low-information environment of this race, we've shifted focus from a traditional forecast to a real-time election night decision desk.

In the meantime, visit the Markets page for real-time prediction market aggregation.

Why Race Night?

I've decided to switch focus to a race-night election calling model because of the high volume of PMs and the low-information environment of this race. There haven't been any competitive primaries here in my lifetime (or even almost two).

— Ryan McComb (@bayes_pr) February 7, 2026
