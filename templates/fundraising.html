FEC Data Analysis Framework

IL9Cast aggregates campaign finance data directly from FEC filings to provide standardized comparisons across all candidates in the IL-09 Democratic Primary.

Key Metrics Tracked

  • Total Raised: Cumulative funds raised during the reporting period
  • Total Spent: Total campaign expenditures (disbursements)
  • Cash on Hand: Available campaign funds for future expenditures
  • Donor Count: Number of individual contributors
  • Average Contribution: Total raised ÷ donor count
  • Small Dollar Percentage: Proportion of funds from donations under $200
  • Spent Percentage: (Total spent ÷ total raised) × 100

Burn Rate Calculations

Three projection methods:

2-Week Burn Rate = (Last 14 days spending) × 2
1-Month Burn Rate = (Last 30 days spending) × 1
1.5-Month Burn Rate = (Last 45 days spending) × 0.67

Cash Runway = Cash on Hand ÷ Monthly Burn Rate

Data Sources

All data sourced from FEC Form 3 filings submitted by candidate committees. IL9Cast uses the FEC API (api.open.fec.gov) to retrieve real-time filing data.

Note: Burn rate projections are estimates based on recent spending patterns. Actual future spending may vary based on campaign strategy and election timeline.
